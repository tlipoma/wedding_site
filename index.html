Travel

There are two regional airports: Manchester, NH (MHT) and Boston, MA (BOS). MHT is about 20 minutes driving from Concord and is a lovely airport. Boston is about 75 minutes from Concord but has more airline coverage. We recommend MHT but it's definitely worth a comparison to look at cost, connections, and arrival times (leaving Boston between 3pm and 6pm on Fridays is a...disaster).

Either way, we highly recommend renting a car, as public transit and taxis in New Hampshire are scarce resources. Note: shuttle service will be provided to/from the wedding from each of the hotels listed below.

Accommodations

Hampton Inn

We recommend the glorious Hampton Inn for families. Situated in a prime location next to the Bow Mobil and I-89, it also has a pool. Rooms are $119 per night, and we have a block under Madden - Lipoma Wedding until June 30th. Please call 603.224.5322 to make your reservation!

Holiday Inn

Not only a lyric to a classic song, the Holiday Inn is also our recommendation for younger, bar-venturing folk, as it's about a block from the bars in Concord. Rooms are $129 for single/double occupancy and we have a block under Madden - Lipoma Wedding until June 30th. Please call 603.224.9534 to make your reservation!

The Centennial

The Centennial Inn is a gorgeously restored inn in Concord. It also is home to the best food and bar in the region. Rooms are $159-179, and we have a block under Madden - Lipoma Wedding through June 30th. Please call 603.227.9000 or email reservations@thecentennialhotel.com to book a room!
