Attire

Sooo...what does "summer garden party cocktail" mean? Great question. Basically, suits or slacks, jacket, and tie for men; cocktail dresses or some equivalent for women; but, above all, plan for comfort and for a warm day! Again, as the entire event is outside on grass, we highly recommend AGAINST heels for women, and recommend flats or wedges.


Bus Info

Coaches will transport guests to//from the Centennial, Hampton Inn, and Holiday Inn. Buses will arrive between 2:45 and 3:00pm on Saturday at each hotel, but there is only ONE bus per hotel, so please be in your lobby and ready to go by 3:00pm, otherwise you will have to call a cab company. Buses will start to leave the reception at 10:00pm and will continue until the last bus leaves at midnight.

Parking

If you are driving yourself, drive past the house and follow the parking signs for "Thomas and Dulcie's Wedding". You will turn into a field (you should be able to see the tent from the road), and there will be a parking attendant to direct you and to shuttle you in a golf cart to the other end of the field.
